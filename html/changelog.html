07 August 2021

DTraitor updated:

  • Machines that were constructed by players now work correctly
  • Updated Ishimura maps that are used by NanoUI in suit monitoring and camera network.
  • TauCeti RnD port.
  • Protolathe no longer supports chemicals in designs.
  • Ports TauCeti drills for xenoarcheology with custom depth.
  • Chemical Dispenser, Chem Master, Cryo Cell, Sleeper, Recharger, Hydroponic Tray, Seed Extractor, Smartfridge, Deep Fryer, Microwave, Oven, Grill, Candy Machine, Cereal, Gibber, Recharger, Lysis-Isolation Centrifuge and Bioballistic Delivery System can now be constructed.
  • Seeker, seeker shells, rig mounted taser, rig mounted flash, silenced divet, and special divet ammo can be constructed on protolathe.
  • Gibber ignores safety check when you try to place necromorph inside of it.
  • Boombox is now portable jukebox and can be printed on Protolathe.
  • Fixed gun (tools) safety.
  • Fixed infinite module stacking.
  • Fixed some slashers dropping their ID because there was no available slot.
  • Fixed RIG modules power draw.
  • Scry eyes and signals cant be pulled now.
  • Necromorphs no longer speak Galactic Common
  • Added science channel to miners headset and supply channel to science headset.
  • Signals can jump to living necromorphs.
  • Added Look Down verb.
  • Fixed bug when you used move up while looking up.
  • Changed a lot of prices in the store.
  • Brought back ammunition and rig modules printing to protolathe.
  • Changed required resources to print weapons.
  • Pulse rounds now require diamonds.
  • Moved CPUs and network cards from circuit designs to protolathe.
  • Changed required tech for power node.
  • Fixed tactical goggles not having an icon.
  • Fixed rock saw design having no name
  • Added plasma saw to protolathe design.
  • Added beaker and large beaker to designs.
  • Removed neural lace resleever and mechfab circuits from circuit imprinter designs.
  • Removed research design and technology data from designs.
  • Removed integrated circuit imprinter from store.

Nanako updated:

  • Rebalanced divet special ammotypes.
  • Fixed several major bugs that were causing spray systems (acid, fire) to miss things in their AOE.
  • Reduced uplink telecrystal quantity from 130 to 30
  • Increased the TC costs of various explosives.
  • Removed conetest
  • Reduced the quantity of event necro spawns by 10%.
  • Fixed being able to move superfast in space
  • Added persistent finances. Your held and stored credits will now persist between rounds. A 10% fee is applied to credits left in your account, and a 15% fee to any you're holding on death. To prevent fees, withdraw everything and stay alive for the whole round.
  • The items in the store are now unlocked with persistent schematics, which can be found in random loot. Uploading one unlocks a thing, and awards a bounty to the finder. Some schematics may be lost at the end of a round.
  • Added Peng.

Snypehunter007 updated:

  • Replaced the old Captain's pistol with a new one.
  • Reduced the Captain's pistol damage.
