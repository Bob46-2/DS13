25 June 2021

Nanako updated:

  • Spacecash is no longer obtainable.
  • Store is now anchored.
  • Fixed infinite withdrawal exploits.
  • Fixed rig modules not installing during store transfer.

22 June 2021

DTraitor updated:

  • Growing a limb in bioprothestic is now twice faster
  • Recyling food to biomass is now much faster

21 June 2021

Nanako updated:

  • Added Stores, where players can go purchase things with credits. Please note that store credit prices are very unbalanced and will be subject to lots of future changes.
  • Added credit chips in random loot, replacing spacecash.
  • Significantly reworked accounts, they are now withdraw only, and at stores rather than ATMs. However, each RIG also contains its own embedded bank account, accessed using chips.
  • Reduced the damage of Divet and Plasma Cutter by 20%. They are meant to be sidearms and not as powerful as main weaponry.
  • Reduced the damage of HV Pulse ammo, from 50% to 40% more than normal pulse rounds. A tradeoff for being more readily available through the store.

17 June 2021

Snypehunter007 updated:

  • Fixed the vintage suit not displaying properly.
  • Fixed the new engineer lockers not having an open sprite.

16 June 2021

DTraitor updated:

  • Added baystation stamina system
  • Added stalk move intent which makes no noise.
  • Necromorphs cant run now, they should use charge to catch their targets
  • Walk is now main move intent
  • Stalk move intent icons

DTraitor updated:

  • Old integrated circuits were replaced by their current baystation variant
  • Added few sounds for IC's

04 June 2021

Nanako updated:

  • When corruption is destroyed via heat effects (fire, lasers, explosions, etc) the scorched floor will block corruption regrowth for a few minutes afterwards
  • Made sure corruption can now go through deployable barricades and knocked-down-vendors
  • Deployable barriers can now be attacked by necromorphs

31 May 2021

Snypehunter007 updated:

  • Removed the ability to make the RIG Mounted Energy Gun.

17 May 2021

Nanako updated:

  • Added the Javelin Gun, a rare and powerful new weapon. It can nail victims to walls, and electrocute them to a crisp
  • Significantly increased the rate that ammo drops appear in random loot. As more weapons are added its been getting harder and harder to find ammo for a gun you're carrying.
  • Fixed shotgun magazines being red

16 May 2021

Snypehunter007 updated:

  • The Asteroid Defense System airlock now works.

11 May 2021

Nanako updated:

  • Exploder's detonate-on-charge now works again
  • Lowered the chance for clumsy mobs using guns to shoot themselves.
  • Portable scrubbers can now be dragged, and many other large objects too.

10 May 2021

Nanako updated:

  • Slightly increased the cooldown times on spitter and puker ranged attacks.
  • Spitter and puker ranged attacks now have a small shared cooldown, preventing both shots from firing simultaneously.

08 May 2021

Nanako updated:

  • Added our first Crew Objective, the Asteroid Defense System. Now fully functional, the ADS can break down, or be sabotaged. A repair mission must be mounted to fix it, but necromorphs will lie in wait.
